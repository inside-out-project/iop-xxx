inside out – STEP 006 | Giving Our Site Some Style – Assignment

Overview

In this assignment, we dive deep into CSS and completely change the look and feel of our website by adding some color, moving a few things around, and completing our menu. As an added treat we take a look at Google Fonts and grab a few to enhance our website even more. Additionally, we learn about caching and how to use Chrome Developer Tools to deal with it. Finally, we learn how to upload our entire site folder to the server allowing us to avoid more actions than necessary and reducing opportunities for mistakes.  Each action item has been linked to the time stamp in the lesson video to help you focus on learning rather than searching for information. Please be sure to complete each step as demonstrated in the video.
